# TikTok 9:16 Vertical Image Production Guide (Universal Solar Stories)

This guide provides 21 production-ready, highly detailed image generation prompts optimized specifically for **TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts (9:16 aspect ratio / 1080 × 1920 pixels)**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **TikTok Safe Zone & Composition Rules**:
> - **Aspect Ratio**: Always specify `--ar 9:16` for Midjourney or `1080x1920` portrait mode for DALL-E 3 / Stable Diffusion XL.
> - **Top Clearance (Top 15%)**: Keep key faces, focal points, and action elements out of the top 15% to prevent overlap with TikTok search bars and status indicators.
> - **Bottom & Right Clearance (Bottom 25%, Right 15%)**: Keep essential details clear of the bottom caption/hashtag area and right-side interactive icons (Like, Comment, Share buttons).
> - **Universal Brand Palette**: Deep Navy Blue (`#061845`), Royal Blue (`#1649C6`), Warm Solar Gold (`#FFC44F`), Silver Grey (`#BFC3CB`), and Obsidian Black (`#0C111C`).
